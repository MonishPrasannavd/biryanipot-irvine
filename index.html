Personal information received by customers will be used only for internal purposes and will not be sold or shared with third parties.

Personal Information

Biryani Pot Express collects personal information from visitors. This information may include your name, postal address, email address, phone number, and other information. We collect this information when you place an order, participate in a promotion, or request information from us. These details are used to fulfill your orders, improve our services, notify you of updates, and respond to your inquiries. After review, the information is either discarded or stored securely.


Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personally identifiable information about you to help us analyze trends, administer the site, track users, and gather broad demographic information. This data is used for internal purposes only and will not be shared with third parties for commercial use.


Payment Information Security

Your payment and personal information are protected. Our Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software encrypts all personal and payment information, ensuring your data is secure during transactions.

  • If the items you received are not as described or not in good condition, you may request a refund within a day.
  • To request a refund, please email us at biryanipotla@gmail.com with your full name, order number, detailed reason for the refund, and clear pictures of the items in question
  • We (Biryani Pot) will consider refund requests if we believe they are genuine.
  • Once we receive your email and the required information, we will review the request. If the request is approved, you will receive a confirmation email.
  • Approved refunds will be processed within Seven business days from the approval day.

For any inquiries or assistance, contact us at biryanipotla@gmail.com or (949) 379-6122. We appreciate your understanding and thank you for choosing Biryani Pot Express!

Updating Your Information
